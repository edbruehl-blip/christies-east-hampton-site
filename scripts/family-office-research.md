## KEY INSIGHTS FOR ED BRUEHL / CHRISTIE'S EAST HAMPTON

1. **Further Lane is the primary target corridor** — Rosenstein, Cohen, Blavatnik, Gagosian all present. Any listing at $20M+ on Further Lane or adjacent streets is a Christie's-first opportunity.

2. **European family offices are the stealth buyers** — off-market, no publicity, motivated by geopolitical hedging. Christie's private client network (1766 provenance) is the correct channel.

3. **Art world overlap** — Cohen (art collection), Gagosian (art dealer), Schwarzman (Christie's patron), Blavatnik (Warner Music / culture) all have direct Christie's auction house relationships. This is a warm introduction network.

4. **Sagaponack / Bridgehampton new construction** — Tepper profile. Buyers in this corridor want modern architecture, not historic renovation. Different pitch than Further Lane.

5. **The $115M Blavatnik transaction (2025)** sets a new ceiling for the market. Any oceanfront property at $50M+ should be positioned against this benchmark.
